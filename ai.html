AI Art
Portfolio

Ever since the groundbreaking paper 'A Neural Algorithm of Artistic Style' was published in 2015, I have been enthusiastically exploring applications of machine learning to the creative arts. From generative adversarial networks to large language models, I have eagerly followed the developments in this field that culminated in the open-source release of Stable Diffusion.

With a deep and abiding love for both photography and artificial intelligence, I have learned to train models that create extraordinary one-of-a-kind works of art. My art is a product of a fascination with the unlimited potential of AI, and I'm constantly learning new techniques and methods to push results to the cutting edge.
